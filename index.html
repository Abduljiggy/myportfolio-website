About Me

Hi, I'm Here To Help Build Your Next Project!

I'm a self-taught beginner in frontend development with aspirations of becoming a Full Stack Developer. I have profound passion about creating websites, with belief that good design is not just about aesthetics, but also about usability, functionality, and that are also user-friendly.
I am proficient with HTML and CSS whilst currently focusing on learning JavaScript, which are the building blocks of frontend development after which I intend to also familiarize myself with frontend frameworks such as Bootstrap or React.js.
I enjoy learning new things, and i am open for opportunities to improve my skills. I strive to create websites that provide a positive user experience and help my clients achieve their business goals.

Aside web development, i possess a solid background on professional Photography with keen interest in digital designs. I love using my creativity to bring new ideas to life. I would enjoy collaborating with others and working as a team.

If you have any projects that you think i would be a good fit for, or if you have any advice or feedback for me as a beginner, please feel free to reach out to me. I'm excited to continue learning and growing as a frontend developer.
